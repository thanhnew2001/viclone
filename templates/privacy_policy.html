Privacy Policy

Effective Date: Jan 23, 2023

The Voices Lab ("we," "our," "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, store, and protect your data when you use our Text-to-Speech with Voice Cloning app (the "App").

By using the App, you consent to the practices described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with the terms outlined here, please do not use the App.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of data when you use our App:

  • Text Input: Any text you enter into the app for text-to-speech conversion.
  • Voice Cloning Data: Audio recordings or samples you provide to create a custom voice profile.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with the App, such as usage frequency, features accessed, and technical details like device type, operating system, and IP address.

2. How We Use Your Information

The data we collect is used for the following purposes:

  • Text-to-Speech Conversion: To process and convert your text into speech.
  • Voice Cloning: To generate and store your custom voice for future use.
  • Personalization: To improve your user experience by saving your preferences and previously generated voices for easy access in the future.
  • Service Improvement: To monitor and analyze App usage to enhance and update features.

3. Storage of Data

All text inputs and generated audio files (including voice cloning data) are stored on our secure servers. These files are accessible to you for future use and can be retrieved or deleted upon your request. We do not share your data with third parties for marketing or commercial purposes.

  • Access to Stored Data: You can access, download, or delete your stored text and audio files at any time within the App.
  • Not for Public Sharing: All data you input (text and audio) is strictly private and not shared publicly. Your voice clones or generated audio files are only accessible to you, and we will not distribute, sell, or share them without your explicit consent.

4. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the security of your data, including encryption, secure servers, and regular security audits. However, please note that no method of data transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as necessary to provide you with access to your voice cloning and text-to-speech files. You can request to delete your data at any time by contacting us through the app or at [Your Contact Email]. Upon deletion, your data will be permanently removed from our servers.

6. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services (such as cloud storage providers) to store and process your data. These third parties are required to adhere to strict confidentiality agreements and comply with applicable privacy laws. However, we are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third-party services.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Effective Date" at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about how we are protecting your information.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at [Your Contact Email].
